Overviews of Skin Diseases

Acne

Acne is a skin condition marked by pimples, blackheads, and inflammation caused by clogged hair follicles and excess oil production.

Atopic

Atopic is a chronic skin condition causing redness, itching, and dry patches.

Eczema

Eczema is a skin condition causing redness, itching, and inflammation. It can result in dry patches, blisters, or oozing sores.

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic skin condition with red, scaly patches caused by excess skin cell growth. It's not curable but can be managed with treatments.

Tinea

Tinea is a fungal skin infection that causes itching, redness, and sometimes a ring-shaped rash. Common types include athlete's foot, ringworm, and jock itch.

Vitiligo

Vitiligo is a skin condition characterized by the loss of pigmentation, resulting in white patches on the skin.
